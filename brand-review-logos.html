05
Construction
Anatomy.
Geometric precision. Every element placed with intent on a 128-unit grid.
Back Square Warm charcoal (#44403C). Rounded rectangle, larger, positioned behind. Evokes a legal document.
Front Square Amber (#B8860B). Rounded rectangle, offset to the lower-right. The active, watched document.
Offset The two squares overlap with deliberate offset, creating depth and layering. Geometric precision on a 128-unit grid.
Corner Radius Consistent rounded corners across both squares. Softens the geometric form while maintaining a professional feel.
