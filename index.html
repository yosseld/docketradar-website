Privacy

Your Data, Your Control

Privacy-first

Your data never leaves your browser

DocketRadar collects zero user data — no analytics, no tracking, no server-side storage of case information. Everything lives on your machine. This isn't a feature we added; it's a principle we built the architecture around.

No data collection

We do not collect, store, or sell your data. Your case information stays between you, the court portal, and your Clio account.

No accounts required

Core functionality works without creating an account. No sign-up forms, no email harvesting, no friction.

Local-only storage

All case data is stored locally in your browser. No telemetry, no third-party analytics scripts, no servers to worry about.
