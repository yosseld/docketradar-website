2. Description of Service

DocketRadar is a Chrome browser extension that monitors court dockets across eight Florida county clerk portals: Palm Beach, Miami-Dade, Broward, Osceola, St. Lucie, Indian River, Flagler, and St. Johns. The Service provides automated change detection, Chrome notifications for docket updates, a unified sidebar interface overlaid on supported court portal websites, optional Clio practice management integration, and data export capabilities.

DocketRadar operates entirely within your browser. All case data is stored locally on your machine using Chrome's extension storage APIs. The Service does not transmit case data to DocketRadar servers.

3. Free and Pro Tiers

Free Tier: The Free tier provides core monitoring functionality including up to 10 monitored cases, 2 counties, 15-minute auto-check intervals, Chrome notifications, and sidebar overlay access. The Free tier is available at no cost and does not require a credit card or account registration.
