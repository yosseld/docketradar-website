About

Built for the daily reality of Florida court practice

DocketRadar exists because legal professionals deserve better than manually refreshing eight different county clerk websites every morning.

Our Story

From frustration to a better workflow

Every morning, paralegals and attorneys across Florida sit down, open their browsers, and begin the same ritual: check Palm Beach. Check Miami-Dade. Check Broward. Tab by tab, case by case, portal by portal. Each county has a different layout, a different search interface, and a different way of surfacing updates. By the time you finish checking 50 cases across three counties, an hour has passed and the actual work of practicing law hasn't started yet.

DocketRadar was built to end that routine. We watched legal professionals spend their most productive morning hours on repetitive manual checking and asked a simple question: why isn't the browser doing this for you?

The answer is DocketRadar. A Chrome sidebar that appears on the court portal pages you already visit, monitors your cases every 15 minutes, and notifies you the moment something changes. No new tabs to manage. No new app to learn. No case data leaving your machine.

Mission

Eliminate busywork so lawyers can focus on practicing law

DocketRadar exists to eliminate the fragmented, error-prone process of manually monitoring Florida court dockets — giving legal professionals a single, reliable command center so they never miss a filing, hearing, or deadline again.

What We Stand For

Our values

Precision

Court dockets don't wait, and neither does DocketRadar. We check every 15 minutes, surface changes immediately, and present case data with absolute clarity. When you're tracking a case with DocketRadar, nothing slips past undetected.

Transparency

We collect zero user data. No analytics, no tracking, no server-side storage. Our privacy-first architecture isn't a marketing angle — it's a foundational design decision. We tell you exactly what the extension does and, just as importantly, what it doesn't do.

Respect for practitioners' time

Every design decision starts with one question: does this save a practitioner time right now? We don't add features to check boxes on a comparison chart. If it doesn't make your daily workflow faster, it doesn't ship.

Our Approach

The Sentinel — always watching so you don't have to

DocketRadar embodies the Sentinel archetype: the trusted, ever-watchful guardian that protects legal professionals from the chaos of fragmented docket monitoring. We don't seek the spotlight. We earn trust through consistent, reliable performance — the colleague who's always three steps ahead, who never forgets to check, who quietly ensures nothing falls through the cracks.

Underneath the Sentinel's watchfulness is the Craftsman's dedication to getting the details right. DocketRadar is a precision tool — built with care, designed for the specific realities of Florida court portals, and refined through intimate knowledge of how legal professionals actually work. We'd rather do one thing perfectly than ten things adequately.
