Features

Every case. Every update. Zero tab-switching.

Built for the daily reality of Florida state court practice. DocketRadar works where you already work, on the court portals you already visit.

Sidebar overlay

DocketRadar adds a lightweight sidebar directly to the court portal pages you already visit. No new tabs, no separate dashboards, no workflow disruption. Open any supported county clerk website and DocketRadar appears alongside your case — right where you need it.

Multi-county monitoring

Monitor cases across Miami-Dade, Broward, Palm Beach, Osceola, St. Lucie, Indian River, Flagler, and St. Johns counties from one unified interface. No more juggling tabs between different clerk portal UIs — DocketRadar unifies them all into a single, consistent experience.

Change detection

DocketRadar auto-checks your cases every 15 minutes, scanning for new filings, hearing changes, motions, and docket entries. When something moves on a case, you see it immediately — flagged, highlighted, and ready to review. No manual checking required.

Clio sync

Case data flows directly into Clio practice management. Hearings, docket entries, and party information sync automatically — keeping your records current without manual re-entry. Connect once with OAuth, stay synced continuously. No duplicate data, no stale records.

Calendar sync

Sync court hearings directly to Google Calendar or Outlook Calendar. Events are created automatically with case details, hearing type, and location — complete with configurable reminders so you never miss a court date. Works alongside Clio sync or as a standalone integration.

Notifications

Chrome notifications arrive the moment something changes — a new filing, a rescheduled hearing, or a fresh docket entry. Know what happened even when you're working in another tab or window. Front-loaded, factual, and actionable.

Export

Export your case data in formats that match your existing workflows. CSV for spreadsheets, Clio-compatible format for practice management. Take your data wherever you need it — because it's your data, stored on your machine.

Dark mode

Switch between light and dark themes to match your preference or system settings. Designed for comfortable viewing during long sessions — with every color carefully adjusted to maintain readability and proper contrast ratios.
