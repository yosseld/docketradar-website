Make sure you're on one of the 8 supported county clerk portal websites. Check that the DocketRadar extension is enabled in Chrome's extension manager (chrome://extensions). If the extension is enabled and you're on a supported portal, try refreshing the page. If the issue persists, please contact us with the exact URL you're visiting.

Open DocketRadar's settings panel from the sidebar, click "Connect Clio," and authorize DocketRadar in the Clio OAuth window that appears. Your tokens are stored locally in your browser. For detailed instructions, visit our Clio Integration guide.
